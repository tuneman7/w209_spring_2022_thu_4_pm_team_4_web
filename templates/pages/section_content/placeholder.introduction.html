Data Science, Spring 2022, W209 Final Project.


With this application we explore trading relationships between the world's top 20 trading nations, (not the G20).

View trade trends, trading block details, impacts of world events, and gain other insights in just a few clicks.

We begin with an interactive overview of world trade amongst our top 20 trading countries. We then proceed to provide visualizations about each trading block and the impact of world events on trade.

Our hypothesis is that the preponderance of world trade is shifting to Asia, with China as the leader of that shift.

Click on the video icon to the left to view instructions on how to use this web site.
