<b>NAFTA Impact</b>
<br/>
The impact of NAFTA is widely debated across issues such as trade and industry growth, positive and negative impact on employment and employment practices, stimulation of innovation, and impact of trade and international relations among the NAFTA countries and with other international trade groups. GDP is one measurement of economic development. The charts below show consistent <b>GDP Growth</b> for each of the NAFTA countries, <b>highlight</b> each country to see growth in a particular year. The NAFTA Trade agreement was also expected to positively impact national <b>Employment</b> levels and in the charts below we see a relative decrease in unemployment for each country since 1994. Also included are charts for each country's <b>Imports</b> and <b>Exports</b> by sector for each country.
