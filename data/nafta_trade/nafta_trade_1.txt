<b>NAFTA Overview:  How Much Other Nations Trade with NAFTA</b>
<br/>
The North American Free Trade Agreement was enacted in 1994 to stimulate trade amongst <b>Mexico</b>, <b>Canada</b>, and the <b>United States</b> and establish a world leading trading partnership. The key components of NAFTA were elimination of tariffs to promote trade of goods and services, reduced friction on cross border investment, protection of intellectual property, and best practices for foreign trade relations.
