<b>Understanding How Much the Top 20 Economies are Trading with EU</b>
<br>
With the rising of China and its role in global trading regime, European Union started to decline in its trading volume against majority of the top 20 economic	powers in the world except China. We see China is taking on a bigger role playing EU's counterparty whereas the rest of the world plateaued or declined over time since 2014.
